About Integrated Youth Foundation

About Us

The Integrated Youth Foundation (IYF) is a registered non-profit organization empowering youth, children, and communities through sustainable development initiatives.

Vision: A society where every young person and child is empowered, educated, and equipped to lead a dignified, productive, and sustainable life.

Mission: To empower youth and children through education, skills training, entrepreneurship, agriculture, humanitarian, and climate action programs that foster sustainable community development.
